Nevada is a part of the United States of America. This of course makes it a democratic republic. This means as a state it's part of the electoral college. It has 6 electoral votes to it's name. The state can be considered a swing state, as it has voted for both republican and democratic presidents in the past. Current governor is Joe Lombardo, and is a republican.
