Did you know?

That the National bird of the state of Nevada is the Mountain Bluebird? Due to the structure of its feathers it reflects light in a way that makes it blue.

Other than that the state flower is the Sagebrush. It's crucial to keep many of the plants in the desert alive due to the shadow they provide.

The state animal is the Desert Bighorn Sheep. Its quick and agile for its size. Perfect for the rocky terrain that Nevada has.

Nevada

Nevada is an inhospitable place, yet it is still sprawling with life. From animals to cities of sin. Nevada truly has everything taboo and tempting inside its borders. Las Vegas being the pinnacle of what people consider "Nevadan". Though the real capital lies far northwest of Las Vegas. The real fun is in the south with amazing views like the night lights of Las Vegas or the giant construct known as Hoover Dam. With a rich history and many chances to indulge, will you let Nevada be your next vacation spot?

Landmarks

Though Nevada is one of the most sparsely populated states in the USA, it still has plenty of landmarks to show us. From the Hoover Dam all the way to Carson City. All these are way different from each other. Carson City being the state capital. Even though it's the capital, the amount of people that live there is 12 times less than in the main city of Las Vegas. Las Vegas is otherwise known as the city of sin. It's set itself as an oasis of greed in the vast desert that surrounds it. Many taboo things are encouraged like gambling and easy to get alcohol. Las Vegas has it all. Hotels, casinos, clubs, everything your heart desires.

We of course have natural landmarks as well, and due to the scorching heat some gotten some pretty unique names. Like Death Valley, the hottest place in the United States. Valley of Fire is another one, named after the red look the sandstone gives it. If you look past all the harshness of the deserts, you can see some beautiful areas. Lake Taho is a perfect example of that. A lake deep in the mountains that has water as clear as glass. From hiking to going on city adventures, Nevada has it all. With the booming tourist industry. It's a amazing place to spend your vacation at.

Fun Fact: The Devils Hole pupfish is the most endangered fish in the world. It only lives in one single hole in all of Nevada. It has entirely prevented building near it's habitat. Even so it has survived a assasination attempt, by a felon with a firearm. Currently they managed to move some of the fish to a artifical habitat to preseve the species.

Places to visit

The Buffet at Wynn Las Vegas

With opening times from 8am to 9pm, this buffet has everything you could want. It's far from a average buffet. You see it's a luxury buffet. With sea food like scampi and crab legs. They of course have a wide variety of base line foods like steaks to! With a variety of deserts, you can be sure to have a grand time at the Wynn.

Bellagio Hotel & Casino

The Bellagio is not only a hotel, but also a casino. It's world famous and the go to place for someone looking a good casino experience. With a wide variety of games and options. Here you can find both alcohol and food. The Bellagio is also famous for all the shows they have. So just know. If you go to the Bellagio, just expect to have a good time. It's always a option to book a room if you are on the strip.

L'Atelier

Normally open from 2am to 11pm as listen on their website. This is a Michelin star restaurant. It focuses on the french cusine. With expensive wine and a abundence of cheese. THis is the place to going if you want to have a fancy dinner. With excellent service and a soothing atmosphere, you cant go wrong coming here.

All of these places are located in the heart of Las Vegas, known as "The Strip". It's filled with experiences and adventures just waiting to happen. So why not walk around and see what you can find? As there is also one of the statue of liberty outside of a hotel called New York-New York Hotel & Casino. Before you ask no, New York-New York is not a typo it's the actual name of the hotel.
